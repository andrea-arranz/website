    Inventions, intellectual property, and income.

    In this specific project, I find interesting the theory that I’ve been building behind the artifacts. Those artifacts are a trial/error of my practice and my ideas to develop my design practice as a designer and project my personal future view.

    Anyway, I think that this kind of project is more speculative rather than commercial, for that, the Berne Convention, which applies in Spain, is enough to have the rights above my “artistic” artifacts saved in a way.

    As I said, taking into account that this project is a reflective tool for me during the master's, I think that the best way to disseminate the project is in festivals, exhibitions, and design platforms. Also is important to me to keep going in the theoretical line inside of this project writing academic papers or applying it as a methodology.
