    Week 01 reflections.

    This first week we were into project management. We have to update our websites and I choose the option of redoing my web through HTML, CSS, and javascript raw languages. I take this task as an opportunity to manage the challenge to go deeper in coding and to lose the fear of using these languages in my design practices. I am still working on my website but now I see the coding as a source of new insights instead of as a trouble maker.

    Second, we have to reflect on the document process during the weeks in the FabLab. To do that, I choose three examples that I found attractive on last year's peer's websites.

  • One way of documenting that I found interesting is the reflection about key points: facts, feelings, findings, and future. Through the development of these points, I can reflect on the impact of the things I've learned in my design practice and for my future self.website example
  • I found interesting the difference between the websites that have photos or videos and the websites that only have a written reflection. I understand better and more interesting the process through visual information. website example
