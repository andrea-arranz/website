    PCB LED

    Week 04 refletions.

    This week has been very interesting. During my education I have made PCBs chemically, however seeing how you can make a PCB with a small CNC has been very interesting. It is much safer even if it is a bit slower. It has also been very inspiring to see how you can make flexible pcb's with the vinyl cutter.

    In the soldering turn I enjoyed the concentration that was necessary to have when soldering. Rei and I have soldered the LEDs in the pcb with the corresponding resistors and with the voltmeter we have managed to turn them on.

    Final thoughts: Generating your own pcbs I think it gives a lot of advantage when you have projects where you need an electronic part, it gives a lot of options when rethinking what a pcb is and how you should look at and treat the "black boxes".
